Reservation Policy

  • Reservation Window

    Reservations can be made up to 30 days in advance. Same-day reservations are subject to availability.

  • Arrival Time

    Reserved tables will be held for 15 minutes past the booking time. After this period, the table may be released to walk-in guests.

  • Group Reservations

    For parties of 8 guests or more, we recommend calling in advance to ensure proper seating arrangements.

  • Special Requests

    While we do our best to accommodate dietary restrictions, allergies, or special celebrations, these requests are subject to availability.

  • Cancellation Policy

    If you are unable to attend, please notify us at least 2 hours in advance. Frequent no-shows may affect future reservations.

  • Seating Preferences

    Indoor and outdoor seating preferences can be requested but are not guaranteed.

  • Walk-Ins Welcome

    Walk-in guests are always welcome and will be seated based on availability.

  • Operating Hours

    Confirmations of reservations are available only during our standard café hours: 6:00 AM – 8:00 PM daily.
